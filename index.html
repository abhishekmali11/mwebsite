Our Extended Safety Solutions

Tailored protection solutions beyond traditional safety nets

Mivan Construction Net

Specially designed for Mivan formwork systems, our high-strength nets provide superior protection during rapid construction. Engineered to withstand the unique demands of modern formwork technology, ensuring worker safety at every level of your project.

Sports Net

Professional-grade sports netting for cricket grounds, football fields, and multi-purpose sports facilities. UV-stabilized material ensures long-lasting durability, while precision engineering maintains optimal ball retention and player safety across all weather conditions.

Rockfall Protection Net

Industrial-strength mesh designed for hillside construction and mining operations. Our rockfall barriers provide critical protection against falling debris, featuring high-tensile steel wire construction that withstands extreme impact forces while maintaining flexibility and longevity.

Green Shed Cover Net

Multi-functional shade netting that provides protection from sun, wind, and dust while maintaining site visibility. Perfect for construction sites requiring environmental compliance, our green nets offer excellent breathability and are manufactured from 100% virgin HDPE material.

About Us

Mahakali Enterprises stands as one of Maharashtra's most prominent manufacturers and installers of construction safety nets. With a widespread presence across major cities and an extensive product portfolio, we have established ourselves as the go-to solution provider for construction safety. Our all nets qualify and meet the industrial standards defined by the Government Of India (IS 11057). Our commitment goes beyond business – it's about protecting lives, ensuring worker safety, and building a secure future for the construction industry.

Social Cause

Safety Beyond Business - The Dahi Handi Initiative

For the past 9 years, Mahakali Enterprises has been proudly providing free safety nets to Govinda groups during the Dahi Handi festival celebrations across Maharashtra. Every year, numerous Govindas suffer severe injuries and even fatal accidents while forming human pyramids. We believe that celebrating tradition should never come at the cost of precious lives. That's why we've made it our mission to protect these brave participants by providing professional-grade safety nets at absolutely no cost.

Our Safety Nets in Action

Thousands celebrate safely under our protection at major Dahi Handi events

Professional installation ensuring maximum safety for Govinda mandals

Our initiative has received tremendous appreciation from communities, local authorities, and media across Maharashtra. Through our free safety net program, we've successfully prevented countless accidents and protected hundreds of Govinda mandals. The overwhelming response and gratitude from the community motivates us to continue this noble cause every year. We've earned recognition not just as a business, but as a responsible corporate citizen committed to preserving lives while celebrating our rich cultural heritage.

Media Recognition & Appreciation

Our social initiative has been featured and appreciated by leading newspapers across Maharashtra

We are honored to serve our community and remain committed to providing free safety nets to all Govinda mandals. Safety is not a cost, it's an investment in lives. Every life saved validates our mission and strengthens our resolve to continue this noble cause for many more years to come.
